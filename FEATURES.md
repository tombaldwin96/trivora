# Trivora — Features for Players

A full list of what you can do in Trivora. **Outthink. Outplay. Outrank.**

---

## 🏠 Home & Hub

- **Personal hub** — One place to jump into Daily Quiz, 1v1, Tournaments, Quick Fire, and Leaderboards.
- **Global rank at a glance** — See your worldwide rank (#1, #42, etc.) and rank change in the header.
- **Level & XP badge** — Your level and progress are always visible; tap to see XP to next level and how to earn more.
- **Friends online** — See how many friends are online and tap to open Friends or get notified when they accept your request.
- **Invite & earn** — Your referral code is on the home screen; copy or share it. Refer 3 friends to earn **500 XP**.

---

## 📅 Daily Quiz

- **Today’s Challenge** — One fresh 10-question quiz per day. Score big with correctness and speed; climb the daily leaderboard.
- **Yesterday’s Run** — Catch up and beat your previous score if you missed yesterday.
- **Days played** — See how many distinct days you’ve completed the daily quiz.
- **Streak** — Track your consecutive days; keep the flame going.
- **Earn XP** — Every correct answer and speed bonus adds to your total XP and level.

---

## ⚡ Game Modes

- **Quick Fire 10** — 10 questions, 60 seconds. How fast and accurate can you go?
- **Category modes** — Themed 10-question, 60-second rounds:
  - History 10  
  - Geography 10  
  - Capital Cities 10  
  - Science 10  
  - Language 10  
- **1v1** — Duel another player. Same 10 questions, same time limit; highest score wins. Climb divisions and track wins/draws/losses.
- **Quick match** — Get paired with a random opponent for 1v1 from the home screen.
- **Invite match** — Create a private 1v1 session and invite a friend via link.
- **Tournaments** — Global and national tournaments; view upcoming events and brackets (Top 16 → Live Finals).
- **Unlimited Quiz** — Practice with no limit; no time pressure, just questions.

---

## 📡 Live Quiz

- **Scheduled live quizzes** — Join real-time quiz events at set times.
- **Qualify to play** — Answer qualification questions to earn your spot.
- **Live leaderboard** — See where you place as the quiz runs.
- **Winners & stars** — Top finishers get recognition; track wins and top-10 finishes on your profile.

---

## 🏆 Leaderboards

- **Global leaderboard** — Compete with everyone; sort by XP or wins.
- **Country leaderboard** — See how you rank in your country.
- **Friends leaderboard** — Compete only with friends.
- **Daily quiz leaderboard** — Today’s top scores for the daily quiz.
- **Your rank highlighted** — Easy to find yourself in any list; jump to “me” from the home rank badge.
- **Profile cards** — Tap any player to see their profile card (rank, stats, bio, country, avatar).

---

## 👤 Profile & Stats

- **Avatar** — Set a profile picture (upload from your device).
- **Username** — Choose a unique @username; change it anytime.
- **Display name** — How you appear to others.
- **Bio** — Short paragraph about yourself (optional).
- **Country** — Set your country; used for country leaderboard and map.
- **Stats** — Global rank, level, total quizzes taken, questions correct/incorrect, total XP, wins/draws/losses (1v1), join date.
- **Level & XP** — Level up by earning XP from correct answers and bonuses (e.g. 10/10 daily).

---

## 👥 Friends

- **Add friends** — Send and accept friend requests.
- **Friends list** — See all friends and who’s online.
- **Friend acceptance notifications** — Get notified when someone accepts your request.
- **Friend request badge** — See pending and new acceptances in the header.
- **Compete with friends** — Use the Friends filter on leaderboards and see friends online on the map.

---

## 🗺️ Map

- **Users online** — See how many players are currently online (approximate locations for privacy).
- **Friends online** — See how many of your friends are online right now.
- **Visual presence** — Feel the community; dots show activity by region.

---

## 🎁 Rewards & Progression

- **XP for correct answers** — Earn points every time you answer correctly (e.g. 2 XP per correct; bonus for 10/10 daily).
- **Levels** — Level up as your total XP grows; levels are shown on your profile and in the header.
- **Referral reward** — Get **500 XP** when 3 friends sign up with your referral code.
- **Rank badges** — Global rank #1–3 get special medal-style treatment on profile cards.

---

## ⚙️ Settings & Account

- **Dark mode** — Toggle light/dark theme.
- **Username** — Change your @username (with validation).
- **Blocked users** — Block and unblock users; they can’t interact with you.
- **Account deactivation** — Deactivate your account when you want to step back.
- **Login method** — See how you signed up (Apple, Google, Facebook, Email, Phone).

---

## 📣 Share & Invite

- **Referral code** — Unique code to share; friends enter it at signup.
- **Share referral** — One-tap share (e.g. message or social) to invite friends and earn 500 XP when 3 join.
- **Invite match link** — Share a link so a friend can join your 1v1 game.

---

## 💡 Feedback & Support

- **Share your idea** — In-app link to send feature ideas or feedback (e.g. “Do you have an idea? Tell us here.”).
- **Social links** — Quick access to Instagram, TikTok, and other links from the home footer.

---

## 🔔 Notifications & Polish

- **Push notifications** — Optional; stay informed about live quizzes and events.
- **Haptics** — Tactile feedback on buttons and key actions.
- **Smooth animations** — Sheens, level-up effects, and flying coins when you earn XP.
- **Safe area support** — Layout adapts to notch and home indicator on modern devices.
